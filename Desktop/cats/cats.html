About Cats

Cats are one of the most popular pets worldwide. Known for their playful behavior and mysterious aura, cats have been companions to humans for thousands of years. They are highly independent animals, yet they form strong bonds with their owners.

Fun Facts About Cats

  • Cats can make over 100 different sounds.
  • The oldest known pet cat was found in a 9,500-year-old grave on the island of Cyprus.
  • Cats spend about 70% of their lives sleeping.
